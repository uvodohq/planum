## Overview

Tooltip components are used to display additional information about an element when the user hovers over or focuses on the element. They are shown on hover and focus, and are hidden on mouse leave or blur. The tooltips can be placed on the top, bottom, left, right and so on.

## Variants

### Simple

In the simple variant of tooltip, you can add a label and children elements.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Simple">
    <Tooltip label="text">
      <Button css={{ width: 80 }} variant="secondary" size="sm">
        Simple
      </Button>
    </Tooltip>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### With shown always

The tooltip will be shown always when you set the <b>isOpen</b> prop to <b>true</b>.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Shown always">
    <Tooltip label="text" placement="top" isOpen={true}>
      <Button css={{ width: 80 }} variant="secondary" size="sm">
        Simple
      </Button>
    </Tooltip>
  </Story>
</Canvas>
